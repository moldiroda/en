About Us

We are a portuguese company that produces decorative and utilitarian ceramic pieces. We are located in the Barcelos region, known for its artisanal activity, in which the pottery stands out for its economic, social and historical importance.

All of our products are unique and produced by hand, using traditional techniques and modern production processes, guaranteeing authenticity and quality in the finish.

We have the knowledge and the market experience for more than 20 years, with all the skills to guarantee quality products and professional responses to all your requests.
